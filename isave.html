I-Save Mobile Application

With the global growth of mobile (smart phone) users, the internet has aided the support of applications for interaction, communication, shopping, researching and bills payments without the use of physical cash. The presence of internet and the use of smart phones have helped in bill payments right from anywhere you are. This has helped reduce the stress of going to offices or banks to make payments when all can be done right from your mobile phone.

There are several nomenclatures given to mobile payments methods, these are: mobile money, electronic money and mobile wallet. These are generally referred to electronic payment services operated under financial regulation and performed via web or mobile platform. Instead of paying with cash, cheque, credit cards, a consumer can make payment electronically via mobile phone to pay for a wide range of products and services. Electronic payment is being adopted all over the world in different ways, in developing countries mobile payment solutions have been deployed as a means of extending financial services to the community. Banks and billing companies now encourage mobile money application for phones as it has aided faster money transaction and it is now easier to purchase consumer goods online and have it delivered to your doorstep via e-payment.

I-Save is a mobile money app that is designed to perform several other monetary transactions, such as fund transfer, bills payment, mobile top-up, save as you recharge, group wallet and it will also help its users to save man-hour usually expended doing money related payments/transaction. Using easy to do methods; it is easy to use, flexible, secure, reliable and second to none. I-Save is designed to be powered by all known smart phone operating system which doesn’t limit it to certain phones, the application will be connected to the user’s bank account and phone number, with this synchronization, transactions can be smooth, fast and easy to track, whilst saving money in users’ I-save account. For Analog phone users, dial *1985*AMT*#.

Non-bank account holders shall be able to do their transaction via USSD code command, using their GSM phone number as their account number tags to effect payment offline in real-time. Transaction confirmation shall be effected by the receiving accredited banks via a verifiable auto-generated USSD text message. Transaction status shall also be confirmed via a USSD query command through the use of PINs or special auto-generated numbers which will depict their I-save account. With ISAVE you can never go broke or stranded!
